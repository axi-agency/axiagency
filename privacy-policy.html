Privacy Policy

AXI Connect - operated by AXI (AI automation agency), Tashkent, Uzbekistan

Effective date: 23 June 2026 · Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AXI ("AXI", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you interact with an Instagram business account that uses AXI Connect, our messaging and comment automation service. AXI Connect connects to the official Meta Instagram APIs to receive and respond to Instagram Direct Messages, and to manage and reply to comments on the businesses' posts, on behalf of the businesses we serve.

Summary. We process Instagram messages and basic profile information only to deliver automated replies and customer support on behalf of the connected business. We do not sell your data. You can request deletion of your data at any time using the contact details below.

1. Who is responsible for your data

AXI provides the AXI Connect platform to businesses ("Clients") that connect their own Instagram professional accounts. For messages you send to a Client's Instagram account, that Client is the data controller and AXI acts as a data processor on their behalf. AXI is the controller of the limited operational data needed to run the service.

2. Information we collect

When you message an Instagram account connected to AXI Connect, we may process:

  • Message content - the text you send to and receive from the connected account.
  • Instagram-scoped user ID - the identifier Meta provides for your conversation.
  • Public profile information - such as your name and username, where made available by Meta.
  • Conversation metadata - timestamps, message and delivery status, and read receipts.
  • Details you choose to share - information you voluntarily provide in the conversation, such as your name, phone number, or business details, so the business can follow up with you.
  • Comments and public interactions - comments you leave on a connected business's posts, including the comment text, your username, and the post involved, as well as posts where you publicly mention or tag the business.

We do not collect your Instagram password, payment card details, or any data beyond what is necessary to operate the messaging service.

3. How we use information

  • To receive your messages and generate and send relevant automated replies.
  • To understand your inquiry, qualify sales interest, and connect you with the business's team for follow-up where you request it.
  • To route conversations to the right business and, where needed, to a human team member.
  • To respond to comments on the business's posts, including personalized public replies and private replies, where appropriate.
  • To maintain short-term conversation context so replies are coherent.
  • To operate, secure, troubleshoot, and improve the service.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your information for advertising profiling, and we do not sell your information.

4. How we share information

We share information only with the following categories of recipients, and only as needed:

  • The connected business (Client) whose Instagram account you messaged.
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. - as required to send and receive messages via the Instagram Messaging API.
  • Service providers that power the service under contract, including our workflow automation host (n8n) and our AI model provider (OpenAI), used to process message text and generate replies.
  • Hosting and database providers that securely store conversation history and the contact details you provide, so the connected business can review, manage, and respond to inquiries.
  • Legal and safety - where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

These providers are permitted to use your information only to provide services to us, not for their own purposes.

We handle data obtained through the Meta and Instagram platforms in accordance with Meta's Platform Terms and Developer Policies.

5. Data retention

We store message content, conversation history, and any contact details you provide in our secure systems on behalf of the connected business, and retain them only as long as needed to provide the service and for a reasonable period thereafter for support and record-keeping, after which they are deleted or anonymized. Clients may set shorter retention periods for their own accounts.

6. Your rights and choices

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Stop interacting with the connected account at any time.

8. Data security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information, including encrypted transport (HTTPS/TLS), access controls, and secure credential storage. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to authorized personnel.

9. International transfers

We operate from Uzbekistan and use service providers that may process data in other countries. Where data is transferred internationally, we take steps to ensure it remains protected consistent with this policy.

10. Children's privacy

AXI Connect is intended for business communication and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.
